ABOUT THE PROGRAMME

The Boarding Hifzh Programme is a carefully structured two-year programme designed for students who aspire to memorize the Noble Qur'an while receiving authentic Islamic education in a disciplined and conducive environment.

The programme combines Qur'an memorization, Islamic studies, character development, and daily practical implementation of the Sunnah.
